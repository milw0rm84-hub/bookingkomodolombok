Komodo Trip 4D/3N

ITINERARY

Day 1 - Bangsal, Kayangan, Kenawa Island
- Lombok Cafe Bangsal (meeting point)
- Drive to Kayangan Harbour
- Sunset hiking on Kenawa Island


Day 2 - Whale Shark
- Saleh Bay Sumbawa (swimming with the whaleshark)
- After Whale Shark, we havw extra swim in other beach/outher sea

Day 3 - Komodo Island, Pink Beach, Padar Island
- Komodo National Park (see them on their habitat)
- Long Pink Beach, also known as Red Beach (swimming and snorkeling)
- Padar Island (hiking and enjoy the sunset from the top)

Day 4 - Manjarite, Kelor Island, Labuan Bajo
- Manjarite (snorkeling with the turtles)
- Labuan Bajo (End of the trip)
