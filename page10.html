⚫Day 1
• depart from senggigi or bangsal area at 5.00 am to senaru by car then get breakfast
after breakfast go to sembalun lawang
• Arrive at pos 2, lunch, rest and continue the hike
• Arrive at pelawangan sembalun 2639m, enjoy sunset, dinner & over night
⚫Day 2
• Super/light meal before summit attack
• Arrive at summit, photo session with sunrise

• spring, swim & lunch
• Going up to Senaru camp site
• Arrive at camp site, enjoy sunset, dinner + spend second nigh

• Transfer back to Senggigi/bangsal area
End of the tour
